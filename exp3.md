**Experiment 3**  

In this experiment, we demonstrate the effectiveness of our method across different number of Gaussian splats. 
Each scene is randomly initialized with uniformly distributed Gaussian splats, and different optimization methods are performed.
The results are reported in a scatter plot, where the size of the circle is proportional to the number of iterations.
This experiment is run on the bicycle, drjohnson, and train scenes from the three datasets.

We did not found any substantial difference for the relative speed of convergence between our method and the baselines.
